## The Story Behind Nick's Success

Nick's journey into cold email marketing started with a vision: to simplify and optimize lead generation for businesses, particularly in the B2B space. With a deep understanding of how traditional methods were inefficient, Nick founded Leadbird.io. His success is marked not only by his ability to stay ahead of digital marketing trends but by his hands-on approach to refining the cold email process for each client.

Leadbird.io positioned itself as a premier lead generation platform, offering advanced cold email automation and prospecting services that streamline outreach efforts for businesses of all sizes.

Later, Nick co-founded Scrubby.io with Erik Paulson and Anirudh Kaushal, a complete email validation tool that goes beyond traditional methods. Scrubby's unique ability to detect risky and catch-all emails has set a new standard for email list hygiene, helping businesses achieve nearly flawless deliverability rates.

So businesses using Leadbird.io aren't just sending more emails — they're sending smarter emails to verified, reliable recipients using Scrubby.

"With Scrubby, users can now validate these catch-all and risky emails, a capability that was not available in the market previously. This innovation allows businesses to reclaim and effectively utilize 40 to 60% of their email list that they would have otherwise considered unusable." – Nick
