This is the cold email formula that actually gets replies. I know you're incredibly busy and probably get a lot of messages, so this will only take 60 seconds to read. That's your opener. Short, respectful, and attention-grabbing.

Then, just two sentences about who you are and why it matters to them. No life story, just enough to make them care.

Next, ask a thoughtful question they can answer on the spot. Can they reply with one hand while getting into their car? If not, rewrite it.

And finally, the clincher: "I totally understand if you're too busy to respond. Even a one or two-line reply would completely make my day." Clean, respectful, and surprisingly effective.
